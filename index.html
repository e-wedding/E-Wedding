Where Tradition Meets the Metaverse

We are a Sabah-based design studio dedicated to modernizing the wedding experience. By utilizing specialized interaction layers, we ensure your invitation is as magnificent as the land we call home. Our mission is to capture the essence of your venue through five signature digital experiences:

  • Botanical Sketch Features randomized, hand-drawn floral patterns that generate uniquely for every guest, overlaid with a tactile paper shimmer that reacts to user interaction.
  • Enchanted Rainforest Utilizes blurred glass panels and fixed ornate frames to create a sense of looking through a window into the heart of the jungle.
  • Modern Minimalist Focuses on precision with a real-time reading progress bar and monochromatic imagery that transforms into full color upon engagement.
  • Luxury Resort Incorporates floating botanical elements and interactive memory cards that shift to reveal venue details.
  • Floral Romance Creates a delicate atmosphere with continuous falling petal animations and elegant framed focal points that guide the guest's eye.
